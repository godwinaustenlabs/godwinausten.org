Result garantie

Our unique Trojan horse technique estimates lifetime values and lead generation potential, charging 5% of each lead's value upfront. If we fall short of promised leads, we refund the remaining value per lead. For example, if we estimate a lead's LTV at $2500, charging 5% ($125), and promise 10 leads, totaling $1250, but only deliver 7 leads, we refund the remaining $375.
