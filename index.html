Last Updated: 8 July, 2024

Our website godwinausten.org, operated by Godwin Austen Labs, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you visit our website or engage with our services. By using godwinausten.org, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Introduction

Godwin Austen Labs (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the website located at www.godwinausten.org (the “Site”) and provides Marketing and Sales Automation services. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information we receive from users of the Site and our clients.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you use the Site or engage our services. This includes:

  • Name

  • Phone number
  • Any other information you choose to provide

b. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URLs
  • Pages viewed and links clicked
  • Time and date of visit
  • Other diagnostic data

c. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on the Site and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Site

  • To understand and analyze how you use the Site
  • To communicate with you, including for customer service, updates, and promotional purposes

  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Credential Management System

a. Collection and Storage

When you engage our services, we collect and store your credentials in our credential management system. This system is designed to be transparent and accessible to our clients via a secure client portal.

b. Security

Our credential management system employs advanced security measures to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your credentials. We prioritize your information security and continuously update our practices to address new threats.

5. Post-Contract Data Handling

a. Data Retention

After the contract ends, we retain metrics and performance results for internal use. All other client information, except what was provided in the onboarding form, is deleted from our systems.

b. Use of Retained Information

The retained metrics and performance results are used solely for training purposes to improve our services. We do not use this information for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

We may share or transfer your information in connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.

We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
